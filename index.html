Who We Are

Filant Consultancy is a premier recruitment and staffing agency with a strong presence in both India and Germany. Founded with a vision to bridge the talent gap across borders, we have established ourselves as trusted partners for businesses seeking qualified professionals and job seekers looking for rewarding career opportunities.

Our team consists of experienced recruitment specialists with deep industry knowledge across sectors including IT, healthcare, finance, and manufacturing. We understand the unique challenges of cross-border recruitment and have developed specialized processes to ensure successful placements.

With offices strategically located in India and Germany, we offer a comprehensive suite of recruitment services tailored to meet the specific needs of our clients and candidates. Our commitment to excellence and ethical practices has earned us a reputation for reliability and results.

What We Do

Recruitment Services

We provide end-to-end recruitment solutions for businesses of all sizes, helping them find the right talent for permanent, contract, and temporary positions across various industries.

Career Counseling

Our expert career counselors guide job seekers through every step of their professional journey, from resume building and interview preparation to salary negotiation and onboarding.

Ausbildung Program

Our signature program helps Indian candidates build successful careers in Germany through a unique learn-and-earn pathway, leading to long-term career growth and PR opportunities.

HR Consulting

We offer strategic HR consulting services to help businesses optimize their workforce, improve retention, and develop effective talent acquisition strategies.

International Placement

Specializing in cross-border recruitment, we connect talented professionals with international job opportunities, handling visa requirements, relocation assistance, and cultural integration.

Executive Search

Our executive search team identifies and recruits top-tier leadership talent for senior management and C-suite positions, using our extensive network and industry expertise.
